Yes, JamesThew.com operates on a paid membership model to ensure you receive premium, curated culinary content from Chef James himself. We offer two flexible plans:

  • Monthly Plan — $10/month: Full access to all recipes, exclusive tips, contest participation, and personalised recipe feedback. Cancel anytime.
  • Yearly Plan — $100/year: Everything in the monthly plan with the added benefit of 2 months completely free compared to paying monthly. Best value for food enthusiasts.

A small selection of recipes and introductory tips are available free of charge so you can explore the site before committing to a plan.

Browsing recipes and tips is straightforward. Simply navigate to the Recipes or Tips sections from the top navigation bar. You can filter recipes by category — Italian, Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, Juices, and more.

Content on the site is split into two tiers:

  • Free content — marked with a green "Free" badge. Accessible to all visitors without registration.
  • Premium content — marked with a gold "Premium" badge. Requires an active paid membership (Monthly or Yearly) to unlock the full recipe or tip details.

Once you are a registered paid member and logged in, all premium content is fully unlocked with no additional per-item charges.

Unfortunately, contest participation is an exclusive benefit reserved for registered paid members only. Non-registered visitors and free users are not eligible to submit entries to any contest on JamesThew.com.

However, you are always welcome to view the active contest details, browse previous contest entries, and see announced winners without a membership.

To participate, simply register and choose a membership plan. Once your account is active, you can immediately submit your recipes or tips to any open contest.

Sharing your culinary creations is easy once you are a registered member. Follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Log in to your account and navigate to your Member Dashboard from the profile menu in the top navigation.
  • Step 2: Click on "My Recipes" or "My Tips" from the dashboard sidebar.
  • Step 3: Click the "Add New" button, fill in the title, description, ingredients, steps, and upload a photo of your dish.
  • Step 4: Submit for review. Chef James personally reviews all submissions before they go live on the site.

All uploaded recipes and tips are associated with your profile and visible in your "My Recipes" section once approved.

Feedback helps Chef James improve the experience for everyone. To leave feedback on a recipe or tip:

  • Step 1: Open any recipe or tip detail page while logged in to your member account.
  • Step 2: Scroll to the Feedback section at the bottom of the page.
  • Step 3: Select your star rating (1–5 stars) and type your comment in the text field provided.
  • Step 4: Click "Submit Feedback". Your review will appear on the page after moderation.

You can also send general feedback about the website directly to Chef James via the Contact / Connect section on the About page.

Once a contest deadline has passed, Chef James reviews all submitted entries and selects a winner based on creativity, presentation, and adherence to the contest theme. Results are announced in the following ways:

  • Contests Page: The winner is announced directly on the Contests page with their name and winning entry highlighted.
  • Announcements Page: A formal announcement is posted on the Announcements page with full details about the winner and prize.
  • Member Dashboard: If you are the winner, you will see a notification in your Member Dashboard alerting you of the good news.

We recommend checking the Announcements page regularly after the contest deadline. Winners are typically announced within 7 days of the contest closing.
